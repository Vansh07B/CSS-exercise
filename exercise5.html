Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

1. Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy, then set aside on a paper towel.
2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place one slice of bread butter-side down on a skillet over medium heat.
4. Layer the cheese, bacon, and tomato slices on top.
5. Cover with the second slice of bread, butter-side up.
6. Cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
